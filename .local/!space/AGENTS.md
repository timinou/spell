# Your space

This is your space to write. These documents are read both by agents and by humans.

When you need to write about a concept and another concept that isn't written needs to be tackled, crosslink the concept and write a template in the more root concept of what needs to be tackled (as an IDEA-tagged outline)

You are free to define and move the ontology, as long as you use the `org` tool, and custom elisp if desired, to do so. If you desire and imagine new features for spell itself, you are welcome to share them.
